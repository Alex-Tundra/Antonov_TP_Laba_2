Vchera vecherom ya progulyalsya po parku. Bilo tikho i spokoino, tolko legkii veterok shelestel listyami derevev. Ya shel po tropinke, vdikhaya svezhii vozdukh i dumaya o tom, kak vazhno nakhodit vremya dlya takikh progulok. Priroda vsegda pomogaet mne sobratsya s mislyami i otdokhnut ot povsednevnikh zabot.
